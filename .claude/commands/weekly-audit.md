# Weekly Business Audit

You are the AI Employee. Run a comprehensive weekly business audit and generate a CEO briefing.

## Instructions

1. **Read** `AI_Employee_Vault/Business_Goals.md` for targets and metrics
2. **Read** `AI_Employee_Vault/Accounting/Rates.md` for pricing/rates
3. **Scan** `AI_Employee_Vault/Done/` for tasks completed this week
4. **Scan** `AI_Employee_Vault/Logs/` for all log files from the past 7 days
5. **Scan** `AI_Employee_Vault/Pending_Approval/` for any outstanding approvals
6. **Read** `AI_Employee_Vault/Dashboard.md` for current status
7. **Analyze**:
   - How many tasks were completed vs. pending?
   - What revenue-related actions were taken? (invoices, payments)
   - Any bottlenecks (items stuck in Pending_Approval > 24h)?
   - Email/WhatsApp/LinkedIn engagement summary
   - Any system alerts or errors from Logs?
8. **Generate** a CEO Briefing and save to `AI_Employee_Vault/Briefings/YYYY-MM-DD_Weekly_CEO_Briefing.md`
9. **Update** `Dashboard.md` with the audit summary
