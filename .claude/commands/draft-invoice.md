# Draft Invoice

You are the AI Employee. Your job is to create professional invoice drafts for clients and orders.

## Instructions

1. **Gather invoice details** from context (user message, Needs_Action files, or Plans):
   - Client name and contact (WhatsApp number or email)
   - Products/services ordered
   - Quantities and prices
   - Delivery address (if product order)
   - Payment method

2. **Check pricing from:**
   - `AI_Employee_Vault/Accounting/Rates.md` — standard rates
   - Known product prices (from WhatsApp inquiries):
     - Organic Shampoo: Rs. 1,200
     - Hair Growth Oil: Rs. 1,699
     - Organic Shampoo + Hair Growth Oil Kit: Rs. 2,500

3. **Create invoice file** in `AI_Employee_Vault/Invoices/`:
   - Filename: `INV_<ClientName>_<YYYYMMDD>.md`
   - Use the template below

4. **Create approval file** in `AI_Employee_Vault/Pending_Approval/`:
   - Filename: `APPROVAL_INVOICE_<ClientName>_<date>.md`
   - Include invoice preview and send instructions

5. **Log the action** to `AI_Employee_Vault/Logs/YYYY-MM-DD.json`

6. **Update Dashboard.md**

## Invoice Template

```markdown
---
type: invoice
invoice_number: INV-<YYYYMMDD>-<sequence>
client: <client name>
contact: <phone or email>
date: <YYYY-MM-DD>
due_date: <YYYY-MM-DD + 7 days>
status: draft
---

# Invoice

**From:** [Your Business Name]
**To:** <client name>
**Contact:** <phone/email>

**Invoice #:** INV-<YYYYMMDD>-<seq>
**Date:** <date>
**Due:** <due date>

---

## Items

| # | Product / Service | Qty | Unit Price | Total |
|---|------------------|-----|-----------|-------|
| 1 | <item> | <qty> | Rs. <price> | Rs. <total> |

---

**Subtotal:** Rs. <subtotal>
**Delivery:** Rs. <delivery or 0>
**Total Due:** Rs. <grand total>

---

## Payment Details
- EasyPaisa / JazzCash: [your number]
- Bank Transfer: [your account]

*Please confirm payment via WhatsApp after transfer.*

---
*This invoice was generated by AI Employee.*
```

## Output Format
Report:
- Invoice number and client name
- Items and total amount
- File path created in /Invoices/
- Approval file created in /Pending_Approval/
- How to send: move approval to /Approved/ then run `/send-email` or `/reply-whatsapp`
